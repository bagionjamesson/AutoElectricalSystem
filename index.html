This Automotive Servicing course is designed to provide you with the essential knowledge and skills required for effective automotive repairs and maintenance. Learn everything from the proper use of automotive tools to interpreting technical plans and performing precise measurements.

What You Will Gain:

  • Comprehensive Knowledge: Master automotive tools, measurements, diagnostics, and servicing procedures.
  • Interactive Quizzes: Reinforce your learning and ensure understanding of critical concepts.
  • Industry-Recognized Certification: Validate your skills in automotive servicing upon completion.
  • Real-World Applications: Connect theory to practical skills, ensuring you're ready for real automotive tasks.

Start the Automotive Servicing module today and build the expertise needed to succeed in the fast-paced automotive industry!

Welcome to Unit 1: Electrical Fundamentals

This unit will introduce you to the core concepts of automotive electrical systems, including basic circuits, components, and safety procedures. Understanding these fundamentals is crucial for diagnosing and repairing electrical issues in vehicles. You'll learn about hand tools, mensuration, technical drawings, and basic diagnostics.

Welcome to Unit 2: Electrical System Servicing

Building on the fundamentals, this unit delves into practical servicing techniques for automotive electrical systems. You'll learn about performing electrical system servicing, repairs, maintenance, and testing procedures. This unit focuses on hands-on application of your knowledge.

Welcome to Unit 3: Electrical System Troubleshooting

This unit equips you with the skills to effectively troubleshoot complex electrical problems. You will learn systematic approaches to identify faults, interpret diagnostic data, and implement corrective actions for various electrical system malfunctions.

Welcome to Unit 4: Electrical System Maintenance

Preventative maintenance is key to ensuring the longevity and reliability of automotive electrical systems. This unit covers routine maintenance procedures, inspection techniques, and best practices for keeping electrical components in optimal condition.
